Google Docs Sync

Automatically sync your contexts to a Google Doc or Folder.

Status: Not Connected

Enable this if your account doesn't support programmatic creation of Document Tabs yet.

How to use

  • Highlight text on any page and right-click -> "Add to Active Context".
  • Use Ctrl+Shift+X to add selected text.
  • Click the "Scrape Page" button in the popup to convert the whole page to Markdown.
  • Visit an AI chat (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) to use the injected "Paste Context" button.
